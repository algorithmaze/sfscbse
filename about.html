Our Founder:

Fr. Peter Mermier, the visionary founder of the Missionaries of St. Francis de Sales, was born in Savoy, France, in 1790. Known for his deep faith and clear vision, he was greatly inspired by the spirit of St. Francis de Sales. In 1838, Fr. Mermier established the M.S.F.S congregation amidst a time of great spiritual crisis following the French Revolution. The widespread indifference to religious duties led him to embark on a mission of spiritual renewal, primarily through preaching parish missions. This passionate initiative grew into a thriving community of preachers dedicated to spreading the message of faith and educating youth. Guided by Fr. Mermier•s spirit, the first M.S.F.S missionaries arrived in India in 1845, where they committed themselves to the cause of educational apostolates across the country. Fr. Mermier's journey came to a close on 30th September 1862, leaving behind a legacy marked by devotion, perseverance, and unwavering faith.

Our Patron:

St. Francis de Sales, born in France in 1567, is the esteemed patron of the M.S.F.S congregation. Celebrated for his patience and gentleness, he holds the title of "gentleman saint" and is one of the most significant saints of the modern era. As a bishop of Geneva and a doctor of the Church, St. Francis was a devoted missionary, a gifted educator, and a prominent advocate for the sanctity of all people. He is also revered as the patron saint of journalists for his extensive writings and use of printed materials in spiritual guidance and efforts to reconcile the religious divisions during the Protestant Reformation. His compassionate approach and inspirational teachings have left a lasting impact on both spiritual and literary fields. Even after his passing in 1622, St. Francis's legacy of humility and simplicity continues to inspire countless people worldwide.

Vision

A holistic society of compassionately human, spiritually enlightened, intellectually awakened, professionally skilled, socially committed, and culturally integrated persons based on the values as lived and taught by Jesus Christ & followed by Saint Francis De Sales.

Mission

To provide perceptive education that awakens creative and leadership skills in every student, to foster a caring and humane society meant to promote the marginalized and to offer opportunities to develop independent research skills, critical thinking, and intellectual inquiry.
