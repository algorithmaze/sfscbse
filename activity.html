Activities

Co-curricular Activities

Dance and music training is provided to students by professional tutors during the school days.

Courses are offered in:
  • Classical dance and music
  • Western music
  • Western dance and aerobics
  • Karate and yoga
  • Skating
  • Folk arts and dance
  • Craft works

Sports and Games

At SFS Public School, we believe that education is incomplete without sports.

Professional coaching is available for a wide variety of indoor and outdoor activities, including basketball, football, skating, chess, and more.

The Clubs

SFS Family has established various academic clubs that offer numerous activities and facilities for students:

  • Science Club: Develops scientific reasoning, competitive spirit, and inquisitiveness through various activities.
  • Literary Club: Provides a platform for students to express their creativity and imagination through literary pursuits.
  • Eco Club: Promotes environmental awareness and respect for nature through activities and exhibitions showcasing students' talents.
  • Maths Club: Focuses on developing aptitude, reasoning skills, solving puzzles, and logical thinking.

Student Council

The Student Council aims to:

  • Provide students with opportunities to develop leadership qualities by organizing school activities.
  • Act as the voice of the student body, sharing ideas and interests that concern the school community.
  • Encourage students who are interested in leadership, organization, and event planning to get involved.
