Academics

"EVERY CHILD IS A UNIQUE AND DIFFERENT KIND OF FLOWER, AND ALL TOGETHER MAKE THIS WORLD A BEAUTIFUL GARDEN. "

LEARNING APPROACH
  • A novel approach to understanding the world through the play-way
  • Each class encourages self-exploration, making learning stress-free, interactive, fun, and concrete.
  • Features a lively layout with attractive colour illustrations.
  • Acts as a perfect tool for skill evaluation.
BEYOND THE SCHOOL

The SFS family offers a range of additional programs for students to choose from, including:

  • Competitive Exam Coaching: For grades VI and VII.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Craft, MS Office, Keyboards.
  • Dance & Fitness: Indian Classical dance, Western dance, and Aerobics.
  • Skill Development Programs: Olympiads, Special art, Abacus, Handwriting (Calligraphy and Cursive writing).
OUT OF THE BOX AND INBOX
  • Class-wise Field Trips: Organized based on curriculum relevance.
  • Social Awareness Initiatives: Visits to historical places and old-age homes.

FUNCTIONAL ENGLISH COACHING AND LANGUAGE LAB
  • The language lab is dedicated to enhancing the English speaking skills of students.
  • It focuses on imparting knowledge of grammatical and lexical structures along with phonics to improve communication skills.
  • Emphasis is placed not only on speaking fluently but also on using the correct diction.
  • Prioritizes the development of English speaking skills to ensure students achieve language proficiency.
ONLINE TEST
  • Online tests are available for English, Mathematics, and Science.
  • These tests aim to develop students' knowledge and capacity in these subjects.
FOUNDATION FOR NEET AND JEE
  • SFS School offers foundation courses to prepare students for competitive exams like NEET and JEE.
KYA & KYS
  • Know Your Aptitude (KYA) and Know Your Scholarship (KYS) are unique programs.
  • They aim to lay a strong foundation for competitive exams, providing a secure path for future academic challenges.
SMART CLASS LEARNING
  • Smart classes have transformed teaching and learning, making the process more efficient and accessible.
  • It serves as an essential tool for understanding concepts and their practical applications.
